Privacy Policy

Effective date: 16 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Accelevate ("we", "us", "our") handles your information when you visit accelevate8.com (the "Site") or subscribe to our email list.

Information we collect

Information you give us. If you subscribe to our emails, we collect your email address. If you contact us, we collect your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we and our service providers automatically collect certain technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and how you arrived at the Site. This is collected through cookies and similar technologies.

Cookies and advertising tools

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Site, understand how it is used, and measure the effectiveness of our advertising. Specifically, the Site uses the Meta Pixel (provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.), which allows us to measure how visitors use the Site and to show relevant advertising on Meta platforms such as Facebook and Instagram. Meta may process this data in accordance with its own privacy policy. You can control ad personalisation through your Meta account settings (Settings → Ads), and you can limit cookies through your browser settings.

How we use your information

  • To send you the emails you subscribed to
  • To respond when you contact us
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site
  • To measure and improve our advertising
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Email communications

Our email list is managed through AWeber, our email service provider, which stores subscriber information on our behalf. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. Unsubscribing takes effect promptly and you will not need to give a reason.

Third-party services

We share information only with service providers who help us run the Site and our emails (such as our website host and email provider), and only for those purposes. From time to time our content may link to third-party websites or products, including products offered through third-party platforms. Those sites have their own privacy policies, and purchases made on them are governed by those policies — we encourage you to read them.

Data retention

We keep subscriber information for as long as you remain subscribed, and correspondence for as long as needed to handle your enquiry. You may request deletion at any time.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@accelevate8.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

Children's privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be posted on this page with its effective date.
