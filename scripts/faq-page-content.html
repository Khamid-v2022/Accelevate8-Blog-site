FAQs

A few common questions about Accelevate and the journal.

What is Accelevate?

Accelevate is an online journal about intentional living. We publish short, thoughtful essays on goals, habits, mindset, and reflection — written to be easy to read and worth returning to.

Is the Journal free?

Yes. Every essay on this site is free to read, and our email list is free to join. From time to time, we may recommend programs or resources we've created for readers who want to go deeper — but the Journal itself will always be free.

How do I subscribe?

Use the subscribe form at the bottom of any page. You'll receive our essays by email — and you can unsubscribe anytime with one click.

How often do you publish?

Regularly — but we don't chase a content calendar for its own sake. We publish when we have something worth your attention. That's a promise in both directions.

Who writes the essays?

Our small team of contributing writers, each with their own voice and way of seeing things. You'll find their names on each essay.

Can I share your articles?

Please do — sharing a link to any essay is always welcome. If you'd like to republish an essay elsewhere, email us first.

Is this professional advice?

No. Our essays are personal perspectives on living intentionally, not medical, psychological, or financial advice. For matters of health or wellbeing, please speak with a qualified professional.

Do you accept guest posts?

Not at the moment. All essays are written by our contributing team.

How do I get in touch?

Email us at hello@accelevate8.com — for anything from a question to a quiet hello. A real person reads every message.
