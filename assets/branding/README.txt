Place your logo files here.

Recommended filenames:
- logo-white.png   (white logo for dark header/footer)
- logo-color.png   (color icon)
- logo-dark.png    (dark logo for light backgrounds)

After copying, also place the same files in:
wordpress/wp-content/plugins/mindful-living/assets/logos/

The site reads logos from the plugin folder, not from uploads.
